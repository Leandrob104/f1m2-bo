The exhibitions of Het Scheepvaartmuseum are diverse and for all ages. Interactive exhibitions for the children, historical facts for the maritime enthusiast, art objects for culture enthusiasts and wonderful stories about maritime history for young and old. Let yourself be carried away by the wind and discover all the exhibitions in the north, east and west wings.
