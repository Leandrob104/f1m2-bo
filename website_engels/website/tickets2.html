Welcome to the ticket page of Het Scheepvaartmuseum. Book the visit date of your choice here. Please note, from the age of 13 it is mandatory to show your personal QR code of your corona admission ticket and a valid ID. In the museum it is mandatory for all visitors from 13 years to wear a mouth cap. With an online ticket you have access to both the permanent collection of the museum and also access to the VOC ship Amsterdam at the museum jetty. There is no surcharge for temporary exhibitions, including the special Willem van de Velde & Son exhibition.
