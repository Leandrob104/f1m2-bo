Willem van de Velde & Zoon

Become a friend

Be inspired by Willem van de Velde's drawing talent! Show your skills, take part in the Teecken competition and have a chance to win a package with beautiful artist materials.

The extensive retrospective exhibition Willem van de Velde & Son is now on display. Dive into the maritime world of the two leading marine painters from the seventeenth century, each with their own specialism: pen painting and oil painting.

As a Friend of the museum you enjoy many benefits and you support the museum by, among other things, making new contributions to the collection.

History of the museum
The Maritime Museum is located in 's Lands Zeemagazijn. This monumental building from 1656 was designed by Daniel Stalpaert as a warehouse for the Admiralty of Amsterdam. The Zeemagazijn was built when Amsterdam was the largest port in the world. Now, more than 350 years later, the Zeemagazijn is still an impressive building with a lot of character. The perfect location for Het Scheepvaartmuseum, which has been located here since 1973.
